Got questions?

Everything you need to know.

Why do I need an umbrella company?

+
If you work as a contractor through an agency, HMRC typically requires your income to be processed through a PAYE-compliant structure. An umbrella company acts as your employer — processing your pay, handling tax, National Insurance, and statutory entitlements like holiday pay and sick pay. It protects both you and the agency from IR35 and other compliance risks.

Why do umbrella companies charge a fee?

+
Running a compliant payroll operation has real costs — software, accountancy, compliance, insurance, and people. A fee covers this. What isn't fair is when that fee is a percentage of your earnings, or when it's buried in a confusing payslip with no explanation. FairGraft charges a simple, flat fee — nothing more, nothing hidden.

How is FairGraft different from other umbrella companies?

+
Most umbrella companies either charge a percentage of your earnings (which means the more you earn, the more they take), or charge high weekly/monthly fixed fees — often £15–£30 per week. FairGraft charges from £6.50 per payslip, flat. No percentage. No surprises. And unlike most, we were built by someone who was a contractor first — we know exactly what bad looks like, because we lived it.

What does the per-payslip fee actually cover?

+
Everything. PAYE payroll processing, itemised payslip generation, employer NI handling, holiday pay administration, compliance documentation, and support for any pay queries. There are no add-ons, no tier upgrades, no surprise charges.

Is FairGraft fully HMRC compliant?

+
Yes. FairGraft is powered by Accentra's Primo Umbrella — payroll software specialists since 1979. Our payroll infrastructure is built on decades of PAYE compliance experience. All payslips are HMRC-compliant, and we provide full audit-ready documentation for agencies and contractors alike.

We're an agency — how does FairGraft work for us?

+
FairGraft gives you a clean, trackable workflow — onboarding, timesheet approval, invoicing, and payroll — all in one place. Fewer queries from contractors, predictable billing, and compliance documentation you can actually rely on. We only process pay once your funds have cleared, so there's no financial risk to you.

What happens when I register interest?

+
We'll reach out personally — no automated sales funnel, no pushy follow-ups. We want to understand your situation, answer any questions, and see if FairGraft is the right fit. There's no commitment to registering. It just gets the conversation started.

Why might some agencies be hesitant to switch to FairGraft?

+

It's a fair question and we'd rather be upfront about it. Some agencies have long-standing relationships with existing umbrella companies and may receive financial incentives — often called referral fees or margin-sharing arrangements — for directing contractors their way. Switching means giving those up.

Some larger agencies also require umbrella partners to hold FCSA accreditation, which requires two years of trading history. FairGraft is working towards full accreditation and will hold Professional Passport approval and all required insurances before processing a single payslip.

What we offer instead is something that actually benefits the agency long term — lower contractor fees mean fewer complaints, better retention, and a cleaner compliance position as new regulations make agencies jointly liable for their umbrella partners.

Will FairGraft's fees stay the same?

+
Our pilot pricing is already among the lowest in the market — £10 per payslip, flat, for everyone. Early partners who register now are locking in this rate.
